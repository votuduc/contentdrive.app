Part 1: Installation & Onboarding

Follow these one-time steps to activate ContentDrive in your workspace.

1. Install from Marketplace

Open a new or existing Google Sheet. In the top menu, go to Extensions > Add-ons > Get add-ons.

Search for "ContentDrive", click the result, and then click Install. You will need to grant the necessary Google permissions to continue.

2. Initial Setup & Licensing

Once installed, refresh your browser tab (Press F5 or Cmd+R). Note: This is critical for the menu to appear.

Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Initial Setup.

The add-on will automatically generate the required tabs/sheets for you. A sidebar will appear on the right. Fill in your Name and Email to register.

Check your Email: You will receive a License Key. Copy the key and paste it into Cell B2 of the "Settings ‚öôÔ∏è" tab.

Part 2: The 5-Step Content Workflow

ContentDrive operates on a "Waterfall" logic. You must complete one step to unlock the next.

Step 1: Define Your Strategy

Goal: Teach the AI your brand voice, audience, and core value proposition.

  1. Human Action: Navigate to the 1. Strategy Draft tab and fill in the 6 columns (Primary Audience, Core Problem, USP, etc.). Do not leave these blank; the AI needs this context.
  2. AI Action: Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Content AI Workflow > Step 1: AI Refine Strategy.
  3. Review: Go to the 2. Strategy Hub tab. The AI has refined your raw notes into a professional strategy. The status will show "AI processing...".
  4. Approve: If you are happy with the strategy, change the status in Column G from "AI Complete" to "Approved".

Step 2: Ideation & Prioritization

Goal: Generate topic ideas aligned with the strategy from Step 1.

  • AI Action: Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Content AI Workflow > Step 2: AI Generate & Prioritize.
  • The AI will read your "Approved" strategy and generate a list of content ideas in the 2. Strategy Hub tab (starting below the strategy rows).
  • Scoring: The AI automatically assigns a "Priority Tier" to each idea: üü¢ DO NOW üü° Do Later or üü£ Backlog.

Step 3: Build the Roadmap

Goal: Move your favorite ideas from the "Brainstorming" pile to the "Production" pipeline.

  1. Human Action: Review the generated ideas in the 2. Strategy Hub or 3. Idea Lab tab. In the Status Column (Column I), type or select "Approved" for the ideas you want to write.
  2. AI Action: Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Content AI Workflow > Step 3: Move Idea to Roadmap.
  3. The system will cut the "Approved" rows and paste them into the 4. Content Roadmap üó∫Ô∏è tab.

Step 4: Strategic Planning

Goal: Flesh out the "Approved" ideas with angles, personas, and keywords.

  • Navigate to the 4. Content Roadmap üó∫Ô∏è tab.
  • AI Action: Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Content AI Workflow > Step 4: AI Suggest Plan.
  • The AI will fill in the missing details for every row: Strategic Format, Core Angle, Working Title, and Target Persona.
  • Human Action: Review the plan. If ready, change the Brief Status column to "Ready for Briefing".

Step 5: Create Content Briefs

Goal: Generate a production-ready document for your writers.

  1. Select the cell of the idea you want to brief (click on the idea in Column A).
  2. AI Action: Go to Extensions > ContentDrive > Content AI Workflow > Step 5: AI Create Full Brief.
  3. Output: The AI generates a detailed brief and saves it in the 5. Writer Briefs ‚úçÔ∏è tab. It includes a link to a Google Doc if configured, or keeps the text within the sheet.

Part 3: Advanced Automation (The "Pineline" Features)

For power users who want to automate drafting and asset creation. ContentDrive can extend beyond briefs into full content production. You can configure these integrations in the Settings ‚öôÔ∏è tab (Cells B10 and C10).

üõë QA & Troubleshooting Best Practices

Avoid these common errors to ensure the script runs smoothly.

  • Do Not Rename Tabs: The script looks for specific tab names (e.g., "1. Strategy Draft"). Renaming them will break the automation.
  • Exact Spelling Matters: When changing statuses manually (e.g., "Approved" or "Ready for Briefing"), ensure the spelling and capitalization match exactly.
  • One Step at a Time: Do not try to run "Step 5" if you haven't completed "Step 4." The AI needs the data from the previous columns to work.
  • Allow Processing Time: When you click a menu item, you will see a "Running Script" bar. Do not click anything else until the "Success" window pops up. This prevents timeouts.

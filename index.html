Automate Folder Creation, File Generation, and Text Replacement in Google Drive

  • The new folders can contain multiple subfolders within them. Maximum of 10 subfolders can be created at once.
  • Customize the structure of the new folders and its subfolders according to your preference. Maximum of 3 levels: Parent -> Children Folders -> subChildren folders.
  • Utilize sequential numbering on Google Sheets to define the structure of the subfolders.
  • Set up the folder structure once and enjoy its repeated usage in the future.
